# Retail Sales Analytics & Visual Insights Dashboard

## Overview
Interactive Tableau dashboard built to analyze retail sales data
across 3 product categories (Technology, Furniture, Office Supplies)
and 5 regions (North, South, East, West) for the year 2023.

## Tools Used
Tableau | Data Visualization | Business Intelligence | EDA

## What This Dashboard Shows

### Chart 1 — Bar Chart (Category / Region)
Comparative sales performance across product categories and regions
revealing regional dominance patterns and category-level variances.

### Chart 2 — Treemap
Hierarchical view of sales distribution across Technology and
Furniture categories showing relative size of each segment.

### Chart 3 — Line Chart
Time-series sales trend across a 9-month 2023 order timeline
identifying a 168% peak-to-trough variance (₹700 low to ₹6,700 high).

### Chart 4 — Circle Chart
Bubble-based visualization showing proportional sales values
across multiple dimensions simultaneously.

### Chart 5 — Pie Chart
Customer segment distribution revealing Consumer (42.11%) and
Corporate (47.37%) together account for ~89% of total sales volume.

### Chart 6 — Histogram
Sales distribution binned across 7 ranges showing concentration
of orders in the ₹3,000–₹4,000 and ₹7,000+ brackets.

## Key Findings
- Identified 168% peak-to-trough sales variance across 9 months
- Consumer and Corporate segments account for ~89% of total volume
- Technology leads sales across North and South regions
- Interactive region-level checkbox filters enable dynamic analysis
